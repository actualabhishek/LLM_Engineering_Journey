# ChatFarm

## Summary

ChatFarm is AITechFarm's flagship conversational AI product, designed to help businesses deploy reliable, on-brand chatbots for customer support and internal employee help desks without building a data science team from scratch. Powered by a blend of foundation models and retrieval-augmented generation over each customer's own knowledge base, ChatFarm resolves routine inquiries automatically while escalating complex cases to human agents with full context.

## Features

- **Retrieval-Augmented Responses**: ChatFarm grounds every answer in the customer's own documentation, policies, and product catalogs, reducing hallucination and improving accuracy.

- **Omnichannel Deployment**: Launch the same assistant across web chat, WhatsApp, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and voice channels from a single configuration.

- **Human Handoff with Context**: When an issue needs a human, ChatFarm passes the full conversation history and a summary to the agent, avoiding repeated questions.

- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track deflection rate, resolution time, and customer satisfaction by topic, channel, and time period.

- **Fine-Tuned Tone Control**: Configure the assistant's tone and escalation policies to match brand voice and compliance requirements.

- **Multilingual Support**: Native support for over 30 languages with automatic language detection.
