# ForecastFarm

## Summary

ForecastFarm helps enterprises forecast demand, revenue, and operational risk using a combination of classical time-series methods and modern deep learning models. The platform ingests historical and external signal data (weather, macroeconomic indicators, market prices) to produce forecasts with calibrated confidence intervals, and continuously backtests itself against actuals.

## Features

- **Multi-Horizon Forecasting**: Generate forecasts from days to multiple years ahead, with automatically calibrated confidence intervals.

- **External Signal Integration**: Incorporate weather, commodity prices, macroeconomic indicators, and other external data sources automatically.

- **Scenario Planning**: Model best-case, worst-case, and custom what-if scenarios side by side.

- **Automated Backtesting**: Continuous evaluation of forecast accuracy against actuals, with automatic model retraining when accuracy degrades.

- **Hierarchical Forecasting**: Reconcile forecasts across product, region, and company-wide levels automatically.

- **Explainability Dashboard**: Understand which factors are driving each forecast, not just the number itself.
