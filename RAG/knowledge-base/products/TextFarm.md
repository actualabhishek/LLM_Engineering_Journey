# TextFarm

## Summary

TextFarm turns unstructured documents — contracts, invoices, claims forms, filings, and correspondence — into structured, searchable data. Using a combination of layout-aware extraction models and large language models, TextFarm identifies key clauses, dates, parties, and obligations, then flags anomalies or missing terms for legal, compliance, and operations teams.

## Features

- **Clause and Entity Extraction**: Automatically identify parties, dates, payment terms, renewal clauses, and obligations across contract types.

- **Anomaly Flagging**: Highlight non-standard terms or missing clauses compared to a customer's approved playbook.

- **Semantic Search**: Ask natural-language questions across an entire document repository and get cited answers.

- **Redaction and Compliance**: Automatically detect and redact PII or sensitive terms before documents are shared externally.

- **Version Comparison**: Compare contract redlines across revisions and summarize substantive changes.

- **API-First Integration**: REST and webhook APIs for embedding document intelligence into existing case management or CLM systems.
