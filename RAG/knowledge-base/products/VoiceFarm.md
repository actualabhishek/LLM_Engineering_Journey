# VoiceFarm

## Summary

VoiceFarm provides enterprise-grade speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and real-time call analytics for contact centers, telecom providers, and embedded voice products. The platform supports low-latency streaming transcription, natural-sounding synthetic voices, and automated quality-assurance scoring across every customer interaction.

## Features

- **Streaming Transcription**: Sub-300ms latency real-time transcription for live call monitoring and agent assist.

- **Natural Voice Synthesis**: A library of natural-sounding synthetic voices across 20+ languages and accents, with custom voice cloning available under enterprise agreements.

- **Automated QA Scoring**: Score every call against compliance and quality rubrics automatically, replacing manual sample-based review.

- **Sentiment and Escalation Detection**: Flag calls in real time where customer sentiment is deteriorating, enabling supervisor intervention.

- **Noise-Robust Recognition**: Models tuned for noisy call-center and vehicle environments.

- **Agent Assist**: Real-time suggested responses and knowledge-base lookups surfaced to agents mid-call.
