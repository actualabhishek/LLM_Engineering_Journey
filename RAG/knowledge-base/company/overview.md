# Overview of AITechFarm

AITechFarm is an innovative applied-AI firm with 32 employees operating primarily remotely across the US, India, and the UK, with offices in San Francisco (HQ), Bengaluru, Austin, and London.

Founded in 2016, the company has evolved from a high-growth startup to a lean, profitable operation focused on sustainable growth and operational excellence.

## Products

AITechFarm offers 8 AI software products spanning computer vision, language, speech, code, data, and security:

### Core AI Platforms
- **VisionFarm** - Computer vision platform for defect detection, surveillance, and visual inspection
- **TextFarm** - Document intelligence and NLP platform for contract analysis and unstructured text
- **VoiceFarm** - Speech recognition and synthesis platform for contact centers and voice products
- **CodeFarm** - AI coding assistant platform for enterprise software teams

### Data, Forecasting & Security
- **DataFarm** - Data labeling and annotation platform for training production ML models
- **ChatFarm** - Conversational AI platform for customer support and internal help desks (original flagship product)
- **ForecastFarm** - Predictive analytics platform for demand, revenue, and risk forecasting
- **SecureFarm** - AI-powered threat detection and anomaly monitoring platform

## Scale & Impact

Despite its compact team size, AITechFarm has built an impressive client portfolio with 32 active contracts across all product lines, serving clients ranging from mid-market service providers to national enterprises and global manufacturers. The company demonstrates exceptional productivity and leverage through:
- Highly automated model training and evaluation pipelines
- Strategic use of foundation models combined with proprietary fine-tuning
- Remote-first culture enabling access to top talent across three continents
- Focus on high-value enterprise clients across multiple industry verticals
