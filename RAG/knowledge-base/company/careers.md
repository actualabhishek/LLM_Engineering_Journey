# Careers at AITechFarm

## Join Our Team

AITechFarm is always looking for talented, driven individuals who want to build production AI systems that matter. As a lean team of 32 serving 32 enterprise contracts across eight product lines, every hire has an outsized impact on our trajectory.

## Why Work With Us

- **Real Ownership**: You won't be one of a thousand engineers on a single feature. You'll own a meaningful slice of a product line from day one.
- **Modern AI Stack**: Work daily with foundation models, fine-tuning pipelines, vector databases, and production MLOps tooling across VisionFarm, TextFarm, VoiceFarm, CodeFarm, DataFarm, ChatFarm, ForecastFarm, and SecureFarm.
- **Remote-First**: Choose to work from our San Francisco HQ, our Bengaluru, Austin, or London offices, or fully remotely.
- **Competitive Compensation**: Market-competitive salary, meaningful equity, and performance bonuses tied to company and individual outcomes.
- **Learning Culture**: Dedicated learning budget, internal tech talks, and mentorship from senior engineers and data scientists.

## Current Openings

While specific roles change quarter to quarter, AITechFarm regularly hires across the following functions:

- **Engineering**: Machine Learning Engineers, Backend Engineers, DevOps/MLOps Engineers, Security Engineers
- **Data Science**: Data Scientists, Applied Research Scientists
- **Product**: Product Managers (one per product line), UX Designers
- **Go-to-Market**: Enterprise Account Executives, Customer Success Managers, Solutions Engineers
- **Operations**: Finance, People & Culture, Talent Acquisition, Marketing

## Our Hiring Process

1. **Application Review** — Our People & Culture team reviews every application within 5 business days.
2. **Recruiter Screen** — A 30-minute conversation about your background and what you're looking for.
3. **Technical or Functional Interview** — A hands-on exercise relevant to the role (a take-home for engineering roles, a case study for GTM roles).
4. **Team & Values Interviews** — Conversations with your prospective pod and cross-functional partners.
5. **Offer & Reference Checks** — We move quickly once we've found the right fit, typically within 2 weeks of your first interview.

## Benefits

- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage (US); equivalent private health coverage in India and the UK
- Flexible PTO with a minimum-vacation policy
- 401(k) / retirement contribution matching where applicable
- Annual learning and development stipend
- Home office setup allowance for remote employees
- Parental leave for all new parents

Interested candidates can reach out to our People & Culture team to learn more about open roles and our current hiring priorities.
