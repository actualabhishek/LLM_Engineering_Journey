# Contract with Global Retail Alliance for ChatFarm

**Contract Date:** September 12, 2025
**Contract Number:** CH-2025-E-1021
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Global Retail Alliance

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Global Retail Alliance with enterprise access to the ChatFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 36 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Global Retail Alliance shall pay $5,610 per month for the first 12 months, $6,264 per month for months 13-24, and $6,879 per month for months 25-36, totaling $225,036 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 42,115 conversations per month. Global Retail Alliance may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Global Retail Alliance receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Global Retail Alliance will receive the complete ChatFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Custom brand-voice and escalation-policy configuration.
- Dedicated onboarding across all requested channels.
- Priority support with guaranteed response times.
- Quarterly conversation-analytics business review.
