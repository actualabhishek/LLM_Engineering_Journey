# Contract with Northgate Retail Group for VisionFarm

**Contract Date:** February 8, 2025
**Contract Number:** VF-2025-E-1003
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Northgate Retail Group

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Northgate Retail Group with enterprise access to the VisionFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 12 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Northgate Retail Group shall pay $7,977 per month for the full 12-month term, totaling $95,724 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 43 camera feeds. Northgate Retail Group may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Northgate Retail Group receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Northgate Retail Group will receive the complete VisionFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Unlimited detection model retraining requests during the contract term.
- Dedicated edge-deployment hardware provisioning and setup support.
- Custom defect taxonomy design workshop during onboarding.
- Monthly model-accuracy review with AITechFarm's applied ML team.
