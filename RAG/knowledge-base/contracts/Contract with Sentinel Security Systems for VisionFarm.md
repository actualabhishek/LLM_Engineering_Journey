# Contract with Sentinel Security Systems for VisionFarm

**Contract Date:** April 21, 2025
**Contract Number:** VF-2025-E-1004
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Sentinel Security Systems

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Sentinel Security Systems with enterprise access to the VisionFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 36 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Sentinel Security Systems shall pay $5,006 per month for the first 12 months, $5,458 per month for months 13-24, and $5,882 per month for months 25-36, totaling $196,152 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 45 camera feeds. Sentinel Security Systems may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Sentinel Security Systems receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Sentinel Security Systems will receive the complete VisionFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Unlimited detection model retraining requests during the contract term.
- Dedicated edge-deployment hardware provisioning and setup support.
- Custom defect taxonomy design workshop during onboarding.
- Monthly model-accuracy review with AITechFarm's applied ML team.
