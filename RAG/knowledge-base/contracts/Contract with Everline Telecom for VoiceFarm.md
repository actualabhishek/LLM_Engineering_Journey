# Contract with Everline Telecom for VoiceFarm

**Contract Date:** March 23, 2025
**Contract Number:** VC-2025-E-1009
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Everline Telecom

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Everline Telecom with enterprise access to the VoiceFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 12 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Everline Telecom shall pay $8,705 per month for the full 12-month term, totaling $104,460 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 30,728 call minutes per month. Everline Telecom may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Everline Telecom receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Everline Telecom will receive the complete VoiceFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Custom synthetic voice design matching brand guidelines.
- Dedicated real-time streaming infrastructure with guaranteed latency SLA.
- Automated QA scoring rubric configured to the client's compliance standards.
- Quarterly call-quality benchmarking report.
