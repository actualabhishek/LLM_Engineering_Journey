# Contract with Continental Energy Partners for ForecastFarm

**Contract Date:** February 11, 2025
**Contract Number:** FF-2025-E-1025
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Continental Energy Partners

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Continental Energy Partners with enterprise access to the ForecastFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 36 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Continental Energy Partners shall pay $6,338 per month for the first 12 months, $6,655 per month for months 13-24, and $7,058 per month for months 25-36, totaling $240,612 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 133 forecast series. Continental Energy Partners may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Continental Energy Partners receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Continental Energy Partners will receive the complete ForecastFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Custom external data source integration relevant to the client's industry.
- Dedicated data science support for model calibration.
- Scenario-planning workshop during onboarding.
- Quarterly forecast-accuracy review against actuals.
