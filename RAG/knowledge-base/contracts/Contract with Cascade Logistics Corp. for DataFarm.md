# Contract with Cascade Logistics Corp. for DataFarm

**Contract Date:** July 8, 2025
**Contract Number:** DF-2025-E-1019
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Cascade Logistics Corp.

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Cascade Logistics Corp. with enterprise access to the DataFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 12 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Cascade Logistics Corp. shall pay $6,443 per month for the full 12-month term, totaling $77,316 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 93,507 labeled samples per month. Cascade Logistics Corp. may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Cascade Logistics Corp. receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Cascade Logistics Corp. will receive the complete DataFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Dedicated, NDA-bound annotation workforce vetted for the client's domain.
- Custom quality-control rubric with multi-reviewer consensus scoring.
- Priority turnaround for time-sensitive labeling batches.
- Quarterly dataset quality and coverage audit.
