# Contract with Pinnacle Healthcare IT for SecureFarm

**Contract Date:** June 25, 2025
**Contract Number:** SF-2025-E-1032
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Pinnacle Healthcare IT

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Pinnacle Healthcare IT with enterprise access to the SecureFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 12 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Pinnacle Healthcare IT shall pay $15,873 per month for the full 12-month term, totaling $190,476 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 5,162 monitored endpoints. Pinnacle Healthcare IT may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Pinnacle Healthcare IT receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Pinnacle Healthcare IT will receive the complete SecureFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Dedicated threat-hunting team access for the contract term.
- Custom behavioral-baseline tuning for the client's environment.
- Priority incident-response support with guaranteed acknowledgment time.
- Quarterly security-posture and compliance reporting.
