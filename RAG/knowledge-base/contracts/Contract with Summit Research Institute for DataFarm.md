# Contract with Summit Research Institute for DataFarm

**Contract Date:** January 16, 2025
**Contract Number:** DF-2025-E-1020
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Summit Research Institute

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Summit Research Institute with enterprise access to the DataFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 36 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Summit Research Institute shall pay $7,152 per month for the first 12 months, $7,650 per month for months 13-24, and $8,110 per month for months 25-36, totaling $274,944 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 145,600 labeled samples per month. Summit Research Institute may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Summit Research Institute receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Summit Research Institute will receive the complete DataFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Dedicated, NDA-bound annotation workforce vetted for the client's domain.
- Custom quality-control rubric with multi-reviewer consensus scoring.
- Priority turnaround for time-sensitive labeling batches.
- Quarterly dataset quality and coverage audit.
