# Contract with Clearwater Insurance Group for TextFarm

**Contract Date:** October 10, 2025
**Contract Number:** TF-2025-E-1006
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Clearwater Insurance Group

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Clearwater Insurance Group with enterprise access to the TextFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 36 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Clearwater Insurance Group shall pay $4,344 per month for the first 12 months, $4,738 per month for months 13-24, and $5,186 per month for months 25-36, totaling $171,216 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 4,461 documents processed per month. Clearwater Insurance Group may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Clearwater Insurance Group receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Clearwater Insurance Group will receive the complete TextFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Custom clause taxonomy aligned to the client's existing legal playbook.
- Dedicated redaction and compliance rule configuration.
- Priority queue processing for time-sensitive document batches.
- Quarterly model accuracy audit against a held-out document sample.
