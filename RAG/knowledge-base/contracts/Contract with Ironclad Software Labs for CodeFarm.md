# Contract with Ironclad Software Labs for CodeFarm

**Contract Date:** August 19, 2025
**Contract Number:** CF-2025-E-1013
**Parties:**
- AITechFarm, Inc.
- Ironclad Software Labs

---

## Terms

1. **Coverage:** AITechFarm agrees to provide Ironclad Software Labs with enterprise access to the CodeFarm platform, including dedicated infrastructure, priority support, and full API access for their operations.

2. **Duration:** This agreement is effective for a period of 24 months from the contract date.

3. **Payment:** Ironclad Software Labs shall pay $7,533 per month for the first 12 months and $8,407 per month for months 13-24, totaling $191,280 over the contract term.

4. **Usage Volume:** This Enterprise Tier agreement supports up to 65 engineering seats. Ironclad Software Labs may request a volume increase with 30 days' written notice.

5. **Service Level Agreement:** AITechFarm guarantees 99.9% platform uptime measured monthly, with service credits issued for shortfalls exceeding this threshold.

6. **Confidentiality:** Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of proprietary algorithms, customer data, pricing models, and business strategies.

7. **Data Security:** AITechFarm maintains SOC 2 Type II compliance, annual penetration testing, and comprehensive cyber insurance coverage.

---

## Renewal

This agreement includes a 90-day mutual renewal notice period. Ironclad Software Labs receives most-favored-customer pricing guarantees ensuring competitive rates at renewal. The contract may be extended in 12 or 24-month increments.

---

## Features

Ironclad Software Labs will receive the complete CodeFarm Enterprise suite, including:

- Full codebase indexing across all client repositories at onboarding.
- Dedicated solutions engineer for the first 90 days of rollout.
- Custom style-guide and architectural convention configuration.
- Quarterly productivity impact report for engineering leadership.
